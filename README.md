# 🇱🇰 LankaTaxMate Pro

**The complete tax & finance toolkit for Sri Lankan businesses — small to large — FY 2025/26**

A free, lightweight web app covering every core tax and finance calculation a Sri Lankan business needs. No login, no install — open it in any browser. Business profile is saved on your device.

**Live demo:** _(deploy on Vercel/GitHub Pages and put your link here)_

## Features (v2)

- **Dashboard** — business profile (VAT registered? employees?) that personalizes your deadlines, days-to-next-deadline, and a full FY 2025/26 rate card
- **Tax Calendar** — auto-updating monthly deadlines (APIT 15th, VAT & SSCL 20th, VAT return & EPF/ETF end of month) plus annual installments and return dates, filtered to your profile
- **VAT + SSCL Calculator** — add VAT (18%), full invoice pricing with SSCL (2.5%) + VAT, extract VAT from inclusive prices, monthly net VAT payable
- **Personal Income Tax Estimator** — Rs. 1.8M relief, 6/18/24/30/36% bands, per-band breakdown
- **Corporate Tax Calculator** — standard 30%, service exports 15%, betting/liquor 45%, with the four installment amounts and due dates
- **Payroll Calculator** — per-employee APIT (PAYE), EPF 8%+12%, ETF 3%, net pay, and true cost-to-company
- **Tax Invoice Generator** — line items, automatic SSCL + VAT, print or save as PDF
- **Business Tools** — loan/lease installment (EMI) calculator, break-even calculator, WHT quick-reference table

## Tax rates used (FY 2025/26)

| Band | Rate |
|---|---|
| First Rs. 1,800,000 | 0% (personal relief) |
| Next Rs. 1,000,000 | 6% |
| Next Rs. 500,000 | 18% |
| Next Rs. 500,000 | 24% |
| Next Rs. 500,000 | 30% |
| Balance | 36% |

VAT: 18%. Rates per Inland Revenue (Amendment) Act No. 2 of 2025, effective 1 April 2025.
