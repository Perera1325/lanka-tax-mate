🧾 VAT (18%) & SSCL (2.5%) Calculator

VAT: 18% (from 1 Jan 2024). SSCL: 2.5% on liable turnover; in invoicing practice VAT is applied on the SSCL-inclusive value. VAT payment due by the 20th of the following month; return by end of the following month.

💰 Personal Income Tax Estimator — FY 2025/26

FY 2025/26 rates (from 1 Apr 2025): first Rs. 1.8M at 0%, next Rs. 1M at 6%, then Rs. 500,000 bands at 18%, 24%, 30%, and 36% on the balance. Estimates for individuals/sole proprietors — reliefs and deductions may change your final figure.

🏢 Corporate Income Tax — FY 2025/26

Companies pay tax in four self-assessment installments (Aug 15, Nov 15, Feb 15, May 15) and file the return by Nov 30. Concessionary regimes and qualifying conditions apply — confirm your rate with your tax advisor. SSCL (2.5% of liable turnover) and dividend WHT are separate.

👥 Payroll Calculator — APIT + EPF/ETF
