Quick tax facts — FY 2025/26

• Personal income tax relief: Rs. 1,800,000/year (Rs. 150,000/month tax-free)
• Personal tax bands: 6% → 18% → 24% → 30% → 36%
• VAT: 18%
• Standard corporate income tax: 30%
• EPF: employer 12% + employee 8%  |  ETF: employer 3%

🧾 VAT Calculator (18%)

VAT rate is 18% (from 1 Jan 2024). VAT payment is due by the 20th of the following month; the return is due by the end of the following month.
